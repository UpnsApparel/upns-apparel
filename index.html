About UPNS Apparel

Your Trusted Sourcing Partner in Bangladesh

UPNS Apparel is a professional garment sourcing company based in Bangladesh. We bridge the gap between international clothing brands and reliable, compliant garment factories — ensuring quality products, ethical production, and competitive pricing.

Our network spans factories across Dhaka and Narayanganj, covering gymwear, activewear, hoodies, T-shirts, sportswear and more. We manage the entire sourcing lifecycle from factory selection to final shipment.

🏗
End-to-End Sourcing Management
From factory selection and sampling to production oversight and export documentation.
✅
Compliance & Ethical Standards
All partner factories maintain BSCI, WRAP, OEKO-TEX and ISO 9001 certifications.
🌎
Global Export Experience
Serving buyers across Europe, North America, and the Asia-Pacific region.

Compliance & Ethics

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your first order with UPNS Apparel.

UPNS Apparel is a professional garment sourcing company based in Bangladesh. We work with a curated network of 50+ certified partner factories. This means you get factory-direct pricing and production quality, managed through our experienced sourcing team — without the complexity of dealing directly with multiple factories.
Our standard MOQ starts from 1000 pieces per style per colour. For some categories like basic tees and hoodies we can accommodate lower quantities for new clients. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
Our partner factories hold BSCI, WRAP, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, and ISO 9001 certifications. We can provide certification documentation on request and match you to specific factories based on your compliance requirements.
Proto samples: 10–20 working days. Fit samples and pre-production samples: 10–14 days each. Bulk production: 30–45 days depending on order volume, complexity, and fabric lead times.
We ship worldwide. Common destinations include the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Netherlands, UAE, and Saudi Arabia. We handle all export documentation and can work with your freight forwarder or arrange shipping on your behalf.
Absolutely. We strongly encourage sampling before bulk production. Sample costs are charged at cost price plus shipping.
Yes — both. OEM: you provide the design and we manufacture it through our partner factories. ODM: our team creates the garment from your brief or inspiration images. Both are available with no minimum design fee for qualifying orders.
Standard terms are 30% deposit on order confirmation and 70% balance before shipment. For established clients we can discuss extended terms. We accept bank transfer (TT), LC at sight, and other trade finance instruments.
