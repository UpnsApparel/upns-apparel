About UPNS Apparel

Your Trusted Sourcing Partner in Bangladesh

UPNS Apparel is a professional garment sourcing and buying house based in Bangladesh. We bridge the gap between international clothing brands and reliable, compliant garment factories — ensuring quality products, ethical production, and competitive pricing.

Our network spans factories across Dhaka and Narayanganj, covering a wide range of apparel categories including gymwear, activewear, hoodies, T-shirts, and sportswear. We manage the entire sourcing lifecycle from factory selection to final shipment.

Global Export Experience

Quality Assurance

Uncompromising Quality Control

Every order goes through a rigorous multi-stage quality inspection process. Our in-house QC team works alongside factory supervisors to catch and resolve issues before they affect your shipment.

01
Fabric Inspection
Incoming fabric is tested for shrinkage, colourfastness, GSM, and defects before cutting.
02
In-Line Production Check
Continuous monitoring of production lines for stitching, measurements, and workmanship.
03
Pre-Shipment AQL Inspection
Final random AQL 2.5 sampling inspection with photographic report before carton sealing.
04
Third-Party Lab Testing
Optional independent lab testing for chemical, physical, and performance compliance standards.
