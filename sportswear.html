Sublimation Printing
Best for: Full-colour kits
Ink is permanently dyed into the fabric — never cracks, peels, or fades. Unlimited colours. The gold standard for sports kits.
🧵
Embroidery
Best for: Logos & badges
Raised, premium look. Ideal for club crests, school badges, and brand logos on training wear and tracksuits.
📋
Heat Transfer / Vinyl
Best for: Names & numbers
Individual player names and numbers. Available in cut vinyl or flex print. Fast turnaround for team kit personalisation.
🖨️
Screen Printing
Best for: Bulk single-colour
Cost-effective for large runs with 1–3 colours. Great for training shirts, PE kits, and club merchandise.
