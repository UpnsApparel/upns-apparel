Fabric Knowledge

Athleisure Fabrics We Source

Every fabric is selected for softness, durability, and the premium hand-feel that drives repeat customers.

🥇
French Terry
Best for: Hoodies, joggers, sweatshirts
Looped fabric on the inside, smooth outside. Lightweight, breathable, and premium feel. The gold standard fabric for athleisure globally.
❄️
Brushed Fleece
Best for: Winter hoodies, joggers
Brushed interior for warmth and softness. High demand in UK, Canada, and Northern Europe. Available in 320–420 GSM weights.
〰️
Ribbed Fabric
Best for: Co-ord sets, crop tops
Textured, stretchy knit with a premium look. The fastest growing fabric in athleisure right now — drives the co-ord set trend in UK and EU.
🌿
Organic Cotton Terry
Best for: Sustainable collections
GOTS-certified organic cotton. EU buyers increasingly require sustainable options. Premium price point with growing consumer demand.
